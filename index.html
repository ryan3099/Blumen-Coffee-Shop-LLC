Our Formula (10 Key Points)

  1. Needs Check: Volume, space, power, and water figured out first.
  2. Right Pairing: Grinder matched to machine and beans.
  3. Water Quality: Filtration plan to protect boilers & taste.
  4. Dial-In: Dose, yield, and time recipes you can repeat.
  5. Training: Short barista sessions for everyone who will use it.
  6. Upkeep: Backflush, descale, and gasket schedules.
  7. Parts: Common spares on hand for fast fixes.
  8. Safety: Electrical checks and food-safe cleaners.
  9. Support: Text/phone follow-ups and quick troubleshooting.
  10. Upgrade Path: Clear options when you outgrow your setup.
